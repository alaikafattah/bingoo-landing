Our Expert Answers

What is Pingoo?

Pingoo is a game based on Telegram, integrated with the TON Wallet. Players can enjoy the excitement of Pingo while earning reward points and prizes by completing various games and missions.

How do I start playing Pingoo?

To start, simply access the Pingoo Game bot on Telegram. Then, connect your TON Wallet and choose a Pingo room to play in. Follow the bot's instructions to purchase tickets and begin the game.

What rewards can I earn in Pingoo?

Rewards in Pingoo include reward points, TON tokens, and special prizes that can be obtained by completing missions or winning Pingo. Collected points can also be exchanged for various attractive prizes.

How to Withdraw Rewards from Pingoo?

Your rewards and points are sent directly to your TON Wallet. To withdraw your rewards, simply open the bot on Telegram and select the withdraw option to process the transaction to your wallet.

Is Pingoo safe to play on Telegram?

Yes, Pingoo Game uses a secure system integrated with Telegram and the TON Wallet, ensuring your transactions are safe. We also use a tested random algorithm to ensure fair gameplay, giving every player an equal chance to win.

Do I need a TON Wallet to play Pingoo?

Yes, Pingoo Game is connected to the TON Wallet to facilitate transactions and receive rewards. You need to link your wallet during registration to start collecting reward points or other prizes.
